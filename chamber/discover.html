Overview

  • Located in the CALABARZON region of the Philippines
  • Situated southeast of Metro Manila
  • Known for its scenic beauty and numerous hot springs and resorts
  • Home to Laguna de Bay, the largest lake in the Philippines

Life

Laguna is renowned for its quality of life, with Santa Rosa City often cited for its excellent living conditions and amenities.

Economy

Laguna is a significant economic hub in the Philippines, contributing notably to the country's GDP. It hosts various industries, including automotive, electronics, and food processing. The province benefits from its strategic location near Metro Manila, facilitating economic integration and growth.
